## Why

Workflow templates and spec cross-references depend on numerical (`## 1. Foundation`) and alphabetic (`## A. Traceability Matrix`) section IDs that carry no semantic information and rot silently when sections move. The same workflow has a `Self-check:` step in `## Action: review` that is a single bullet under eleven, with no marker, no audit hook, and no pre-merge gate ‚Äî it gets skipped without anyone noticing (caught only by user reminder during PR #50). Bundling fixes both: one `template-version`-Bump cascade, one consumer merge prompt, one audit pass.
